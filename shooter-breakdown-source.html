NW Section 2019 - Shooter Breakdown
Information about shooter attendance, division and class breakdown can be found below.

TotalUniqueShooters - A shooter will count against this total if they shot in a division or class at one or more matches this season.
TotalEligibleShooters - A shooter will count against this total if they had enough section scores to count for the series.
TotalEligibleSectionShooters - A shooter will count against this total if they had enough section scores to count for the series AND they are a member of the section.
