Links to the NW Section Results and Awards can be found below.

Qualification Note: This year, there was a required minimum of 3 scoring section matches to qualify for a section score. Section scores are calculated by taking a shooter's top 3 overall division percentages and averaging them. Standings are then determined using this averaged score.

If you have any questions on how your score was calculated, the excel document has sheet called 'FinalStanding-All'. This is an unfiltered and unsorted list of all shooters and their section scores. It also contains detailed information about which match scores were used in the section score calculation.
